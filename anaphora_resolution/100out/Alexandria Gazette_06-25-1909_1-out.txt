Rev. Joseph Hardy, a blind Negro preacher, was lynched sometime Wednesday night near Taalbotton, Ga., because it is alleged a blind preacher was stirring up strife between the races. The lynching of the blind preacher is closely connected with the killing of the Negro, Will Caneker, by a mob Wednesday morning. Caneker was lynched for killing William Leonard, a prominent planter, it is alleged a blind a blind preacher instigated that crime.
